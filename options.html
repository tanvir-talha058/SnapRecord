About

SnapRecord is a lightweight screen recording extension for Chromium-based browsers. It provides an easy way to capture your screen, browser tabs, or application windows with optional audio recording.

Version: 1.0.0
Features: Screen/Tab/Window recording, Microphone & System audio, Pause/Resume
