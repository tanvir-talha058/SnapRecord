About

SnapRecord - Version 1.0.0

A lightweight screen recording extension for Chromium-based browsers

Features:

  • Record tabs, windows, or entire screen
  • High-quality video recording (up to 1440p)
  • System audio and microphone support
  • Pause and resume recordings
  • Lightweight with no external dependencies
